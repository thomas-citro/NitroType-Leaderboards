üîíSpecial Requirements

In order to promote fairness of the leaderboards, I have some special requirements for users/teams to be included:

  • Player Leaderboards
    • For ALL Player Leaderboards:
      • Minimum 1,000 alltime races.
      • For non-gold players: Minimum 5 months old.
      • For gold players: Minimum 2 months old.
      • Must not be banned from the leaderboards. Here's why a player might be banned from ntleaderboards:
        1. Player was banned by Nitro Type in the past.
        2. Player was a captain of a team that was banned from ntleaderboards.
        3. Account was traded, sold, or stolen.
        4. Player was detected of botting via algorithms that analyze user data.
    • Top NM Racers (Season):
      • Minimum season average score of 13.
    • Highest Scoring Racers (Season):
      • Minimum 15 season games.
    • Fastest Racers (Alltime):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
    • Fastest Racers (Season):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
      • Minimum 100 season races.
    • Fastest Racers (Last 7 Days):
      • Minimum 50 races in the last 7 days.
    • Fastest Racers (Last 24 Hours):
      • Minimum 20 races in the last 24 hours.
    • Most Accurate Racers (Alltime):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
    • Most Accurate Racers (Season):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
      • Minimum 100 season races.
    • Most Accurate Racers (Last 7 Days):
      • Minimum 50 races in the last 7 days.
    • Most Accurate Racers (Last 24 Hours):
      • Minimum 20 races in the last 24 hours.
    • Most Races Per Day:
      • Minimum 5 months old (including gold members).
    • Oldest Active Players:
      • Minimum level 2 for the season.
    • Oldest Active Team Members:
      • Minimum level 2 for the season.
  • Team Leaderboards
    • For ALL NT Team Leaderboards:
      • Minimum 1,000 alltime races.
      • Minimum 1 month old.
      • Must not be banned from the leaderboards. Here's why a team might be banned from ntleaderboards:
        1. Team captain was banned from ntleaderboards.
        2. Team was detected of widespread botting via algorithms that analyze team data.
        3. Team captain failed to kick flagged botters from the team.
    • Most Active NM Teams:
      • Minimum alltime average score of 10.
    • Top NM Teams (Season):
      • Minimum season average score of 10.
    • Highest Scoring NM Teams (Season):
      • Minimum 10 team members.
      • Minimum 15 season games.
      • Minimum 25% of team active.
    • Fastest Teams (Season):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
      • Minimum 300 season races.
      • Minimum 15 team members.
      • Minimum 35% of team active.
    • Fastest Teams (Alltime):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
      • Minimum 15 team members.
      • Minimum 10 team members with 100+ team races.
    • Most Accurate Teams (Season):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
      • Minimum 300 season races.
      • Minimum 15 team members.
      • Minimum 35% of team active.
    • Most Accurate Teams (Alltime):
      • Minimum 2,000 alltime races.
      • Minimum 15 team members.
      • Minimum 10 team members with 100+ team races.
    • Most Races Per Day:
      • Minimum 3 months old.
      • Minimum 10 team members.
    • Oldest Active NT Teams:
      • Minimum 100 season races.
      • Minimum 10 team members.
      • Minimum 30% of team active.
    • Oldest Active NM Teams:
      • Minimum 20 season games.
      • Minimum 10 team members.
      • Minimum 20% of team active.
