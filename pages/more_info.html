Special Requirements

In order to promote fairness of the leaderboards, I have employed some special requirements for users/teams to be included:

  • Fastest Racers (Season)
    • Can't be over 250 average wpm.
    • Must have at least 500 alltime races.
    • Must have at least 100 season races.
    • Account must be at least a month old.
  • Fastest Racers (Alltime)
    • Can't be over 250 average wpm.
    • Must have at least 500 alltime races.
    • Account must be at least a month old.
  • Fastest Teams (Season)
    • Can't be over 250 average wpm.
    • Must have at least 10 members on team.
    • Must have at least 1,000 alltime races.
    • Must have at least 300 season races.
    • Team must be at least a month old.
  • Fastest Teams (Alltime)
    • Can't be over 250 average wpm.
    • Must have at least 10 members on team.
    • Must have at least 1,000 alltime races.
    • Team must be at least a month old.
  • Longest Session
    • Account must be at least a month old.
  • Oldest Active Players
    • To be considered active, a player must be higher than level 1 for the season.
